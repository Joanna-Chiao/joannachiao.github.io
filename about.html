About Joanna關於趙喬安娜

A Decade of Trusted Expertise十年值得信賴的專業

I have spent over 10 years studying and working in the UK financial sector, advising individuals, families, business owners, and internationally connected households. My experience across small, mid-sized, and large financial institutions has given me a practical understanding of how financial planning operates in reality — not merely in theory.

In addition to private client advisory work, I have delivered investment, retirement, and wealth planning sessions for academics and professionals from institutions including Imperial College London, the London School of Economics (LSE), and University College London (UCL).

My work centres on tax efficiency, long-term financial security, family wealth preservation, and the legal and structural considerations surrounding these areas. I take a structured and tailored approach, ensuring clients fully understand their options so that decisions are made with clarity and conviction.

I am currently continuing my studies in Political Economy at the University of Oxford. I believe financial planning cannot be separated from broader economic and political forces — this perspective enables me to help clients plan not only for wealth accumulation, but for long-term resilience and stability.

I also volunteer at The Conduit Club, supporting financial education initiatives for younger generations. I believe financial awareness should begin early and develop into lifelong discipline.

我在英國金融界深耕逾十年，為個人、家庭、企業主及跨國家庭提供專業建議。在大、中、小型金融機構的廣泛經歷，使我對財務規劃的實際運作有深刻且務實的理解——而非僅止於理論。

除私人客戶顧問工作外，我亦曾在倫敦帝國理工學院、倫敦政治經濟學院（LSE）及大學學院倫敦（UCL）為學者及專業人士主講投資、退休及財富規劃課程。

我的工作核心涵蓋稅務效率、長期財務保障、家族財富傳承，以及相關的法律與架構考量。我採用結構化、個人化的方式，確保客戶充分了解所有選項，讓每個決策都建立在清晰與信念之上。

我目前正於牛津大學修讀政治經濟學。我深信財務規劃與更廣泛的經濟及政治力量密不可分——這一視角使我能協助客戶不僅規劃財富增長，更建立長遠的穩定性與抗禦力。

我亦在The Conduit Club擔任義工，支持面向年輕一代的財務教育項目。我相信財務意識應從早培養，並發展成終身的紀律。
